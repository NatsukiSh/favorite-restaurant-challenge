My Favorite Restaurant Sugakiya

About Sugakiya

To begin with, we need to talk about "Nagoya Meshi" (It is in Japanese as Nagoya food). Nagoya Meshi is a Japanese regional cuisine of the city of Nagoya and the surrounding region in cental Japan. And Sugakiya is the one of chains in Nagoya. It is well-known as reasonable and tasty. Teenagers can stop by to eat with friends after school. If you eat here, you can experience and feel real Nagoya food. It is a taste that I have known since childhood and is always a welcome relief.
